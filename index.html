Case Study #1: Contact Centre Simulation & Optimization

Problem: A company owns large call centers with highly complex infrastructures and sophisticated process management. The company's representatives wanted to create a call center simulation model in order to optimize the headcount and the scheduling and routing of calls, by using aggregated data. These steps were aimed at improving call center overall performance and customer satisfaction rates, as well as lowering the abandonment rate.

Solution: A simulation model was built using AnyLogic that incorporated calls, call center representatives and their skills, routing and abandonments in detail. The insights which were gained from the call center simulation and optimization acted as a basis for some process improvement ideas.

Case Study #2: Product Recommender

Problem: A company is looking to optimize online sales by recommending products most likely to be sold to customers.

Solution: A recommender engine was built using PySpark on an AWS Stack.

Case Study #3: Stock Market Trends Analysis

Problem: A stock market investor wants a holistic look at recent movmements of TSX/S&P stocks.

Solution: Python API calls were made to collect historical stock data, storing it on Microsoft Azure and displaying results in Power BI.

Case Study #4: US Election Topic & Trends Analysis

Problem: A social media marketing campaign manager for a political figure wants to measure its candidate's performance on key topics.

Solution: A Python API call was used to collect Twitter data from Trump, Biden, Pence and Harris accounts. Key topics were identified along with their popularity using Natural Language Processing (NLP).
